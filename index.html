From MUN Co-ordinator's Desk

Greetings!

Rabindranath Tagore had envisaged a world where the mind is without fear and the head is held high, a world that has not been broken up into fragments, world where peace prevails. This is the true essence of MUN, to prepare global citizens of tomorrow who can help draft a world that is democratic, united and fraternised. We need to create a universal fabric - seamless and smooth braided with patterns and colours of harmony and unison. It is thus my honour and distinct pleasure to invite young delegates to the 3rd edition of the Seth Anandram Jaipuria School Model United Nations- Jaipuria MUNC’21 on 13th and 14th August, where we shall be focusing on issues meritingcontemporary relevance, which are in tow with the leanings of the United Nations.

This edition of Jaipuria MUNC’21 is being organised under the theme: “Sapientia | Pax | Fraternitas” - Wisdom, Peace, Fraternity. It is in the spirit of this theme that our conference strives to promote policy consensus through discussions and debate rather than conflict and war. In times of great geopolitical volatility, the need of the hour is to inculcate a practice wherein the leaders of tomorrow engage in multilateral discourse and develop a concord after discussion and conflict resolution. I would like to extend my gratitude to our Shri Shishir Jaipuria, Chairman Seth Anandram Jaipuria Education Society and Mrs. Manju Rana, Principal cum Director, for always providing us the vision to work for a better tomorrow and by setting high goals for all of us by their dynamic leadership. It is only through their unwavering support and guidance that this inaugural effort could reach the zenith of success.

With heartfelt appreciation, I would like to recognise the efforts of the Alumni Members, the Secretariat and the Organising Committee Members who have worked so unstintingly and efficiently to lay the foundation ground of this conference. We look forward to your enthusiastic participation and await to host you all at Jaipuria MUNC’21.
Best Wishes !

Sumita Vij

From Prinicpal's Desk

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors

Today, we live in a world that is rife with conflict and strife amongst nations. Hostilities – whether overt or covert – divert the already scarce resources away from any nation’s developmental goals into unproductive, wasteful channels with no fruitful outcome. The need of the hour is to bring about the youth to a common forum, where the conflicts can be resolved through engagement, dialogue and mutual understanding.

We, at Seth Anandran Jaipuria School, have always welcomed avenues that would allow our young, dynamic leaders to widen their horizons beyond mere textbook knowledge and venture into the less explored territories of International Politics and Policy Making. This year we are launching our 3rd Edition of Jaipuria MUNC - 2021 with the motto - “Sapientia | Pax | Fraternitas” - Wisdom, Peace, Fraternity and we hope to provide a unique opportunity to engage students in more productive dialogues, while developing strong friendship bonds. The MUN is an excellent platform that nurtures young minds to grow into analytical and global citizens with the aim to change the world for the better. The conference will draw attention not only to a sensitive issue of social cohesion but also issues of global importance through different committees. The focus will be on concrete solutions and ways to make our world a better and more secure place to live. Their path of hope, courage, honesty and hard work will create a world whose ‘golden age’ is not beyond but ahead of us.

I cordially invite you all, to be a part of this esteemed Jaipuria MUNC’21 and sincerely look forward to offer you a warm hospitality and tremendous support in making this conference an incredible experience. I also extend my best wishes to the delegates and greetings to one and all.
Warm regards

Manju Rana
